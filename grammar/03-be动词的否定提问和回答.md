# be动词的否定提问和回答


## be动词的否定

    在am, is, are, was, were后面加not，缩略式：am not, isn't, aren't, wasn't, weren't

### Example

    The man isn't back.
    I am not back.
    They aren't back.
    He wasn't back.
    They weren't back.

## 使用be动词提问和回答


    Is he a teacher ?
    Yes, he is.
    No, he isn't.

    Are you a teacher ?
    Yes, I am.
    No, I am not.
    
    Were they teachers ?
    Yes, they were.
    No, they weren't.
